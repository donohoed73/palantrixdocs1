<Info>
  First time users need to sign up to create their Palantrix account. For additional protection set a secure password that you do not use on any other site.
</Info>

### Create Your Account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up to Palantrix">
    Please visit [my.palantrix.com](http://my.palantrix.com) and click on the Sign Up link.

    <img
      src="/Pal-Signup1.png"
      alt="image.png"
      title="image.png"
      className="mr-auto"
      style={{ width:"45%" }}
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add your Email">
    Enter your email address and press Continue.

    <img
      src="/images/Pal-Signup2.png"
      alt="Pal Signup2 Pn"
      title="Pal Signup2 Pn"
      style={{ width:"45%" }}
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your OTP">
    An One-Time-Passcode (OTP) will be sent to your email address. Please open your email and retrieve the 6-digit code and enter it in the OTP fields and click Verify & Continue.

    <Check>
      If you have not received your OTP email, please check your SPAM or JUNK folders
    </Check>
    <img
      src="/images/Pal-Signup3.png"
      alt="Pal Signup3 Pn"
      title="Pal Signup3 Pn"
      style={{ width:"47%" }}
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter and Confirm your password">
    Enter in a strong password. We validate that you use at least:

    1. Password must be at least 8 characters long
    2. One uppercase letter
    3. One lowercase letter
    4. One number
    5. One special character e.g. !, &, %, \$, @, +, \*, ?

    <img
      src="/images/Pal-Signup4.png"
      alt="Pal Signup4 Pn"
      title="Pal Signup4 Pn"
      style={{ width:"48%" }}
    />
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Quick Tips

- Use an email that is easily accessible to retrieve your OTP and complete your verification.
- If you have already created an account just use the Login option on the same page.
- If the prompt tells you that your password criteria has not been met, just check Step 4 above to ensure that the all 5 criteria are being met.
