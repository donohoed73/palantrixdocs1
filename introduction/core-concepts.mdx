# Core Concepts

This guide explains the fundamental components and concepts that make up the Ravan.ai platform.

## AI Agents

AI Agents are the core of Ravan.ai's platform. They are intelligent conversational interfaces that can:

- Engage in natural, human-like conversations
- Understand context and maintain conversation flow
- Handle multiple interaction channels (phone, web, video)
- Learn and improve over time

### Types of Agents

1. **Phone Agents**
   - Handle inbound and outbound calls
   - Support multiple phone numbers
   - Integrate with existing phone systems

2. **Web Agents**
   - Provide chat support on websites
   - Support video interactions
   - Customizable appearance and behavior

3. **Hybrid Agents**
   - Seamlessly switch between channels
   - Maintain context across interactions
   - Provide consistent experience

## Conversation Engine

The Conversation Engine powers the natural interactions of our AI agents:

### Key Features
- **Natural Language Understanding**: Advanced NLP for context awareness
- **Speech Recognition**: Clear understanding even in noisy environments
- **Response Generation**: Human-like, contextually appropriate responses
- **Sentiment Analysis**: Real-time understanding of customer emotions
- **Memory**: Persistent context across conversations

### Performance
- <500ms response times
- 99.9% uptime
- Multi-language support
- Noise cancellation
- Speaker isolation

## Campaigns

Campaigns are structured sequences of interactions designed to achieve specific business goals:

### Types of Campaigns
1. **Outbound Campaigns**
   - Lead generation
   - Appointment scheduling
   - Follow-up sequences

2. **Inbound Campaigns**
   - Customer support
   - Lead qualification
   - Information gathering

### Campaign Features
- Customizable call scripts
- Dynamic branching logic
- Performance tracking
- A/B testing capabilities
- Integration with CRMs
