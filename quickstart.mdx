# Quickstart Guide

This guide will help you get started with Ravan.ai quickly. We'll walk you through the essential steps to set up your first AI agent.

## Prerequisites

- A Ravan.ai account (Sign up at [app.ravan.ai](https://app.ravan.ai))
- A phone number (if you plan to use phone agents)
- A website (if you plan to use web agents)

## Step 1: Schedule Your AI Setup Call

Our unique onboarding process begins with an AI setup call where we'll:

1. Understand your business needs
2. Configure your AI agent's personality
3. Set up your initial use cases

<Callout type="info">
  The AI setup call typically takes 30-45 minutes and is essential for optimal agent performance.
</Callout>

## Step 2: Configure Your First Agent

After your setup call, you'll be ready to configure your first agent:

1. Log in to your [Ravan.ai Dashboard](https://app.ravan.ai)
2. Navigate to "Agents" in the sidebar
3. Click "Create New Agent"
4. Choose your agent type (Phone or Web)
5. Follow the configuration wizard

## Step 3: Test Your Agent

Before going live, test your agent:

1. For phone agents: Call your test number
2. For web agents: Use the preview mode
3. Verify the conversation flow
4. Check response quality

## Step 4: Go Live

Once you're satisfied with testing:

1. For phone agents: Link your business number
2. For web agents: Add the widget to your website
3. Monitor initial interactions
4. Make adjustments as needed
