Our platform streamlines corporate hiring, offering tools like AI job postings and Palantrix Shorts to efficiently screen talent. From initial screening to final hire, Palantrix delivers a seamless, end-to-end recruitment solution that drives success.

Palantrix harnesses AI to supercharge, not supplant, human decisions, revolutionising initial candidate screening. Our Palantrix Shorts feature delivers vibrant video intros that outshine static and outdated CVs.
